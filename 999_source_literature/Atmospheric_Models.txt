%% Logarithmic wind profile %%

For Neutral atmospheric conditions: 
Logarithmic law as described on p.16 in 	Nielsen, F. G.: Offshore Wind Energy, Environmental Conditions and Dynamics of Fixed and Floating Turbines. New York, Unites States: Cambridge University Press, 2024. 
											isbn: 9781009341455. doi: 10.1017/9781009341455.

u(z) = (u_ambient*log(z/z_Surf))/log(z_r/z_Surf)
